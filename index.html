About Logic Spark Entertainment

Logic Spark Entertainment is a youth-centered creative and development company focused on nurturing talent, innovation, and opportunity through entertainment, education, and professional exposure.

We exist to bridge the gap between young creatives and corporate institutions by creating platforms where talent is discovered, trained, and connected to real-world opportunities.

What We Do

  • Organize corporate and youth-focused events
  • Host music shows, festivals, and live performances
  • Run mentorship and talent development programs
  • Produce digital media and creative content
  • Create employment and networking opportunities

Our Vision

To become a leading entertainment and youth empowerment brand in Africa by building sustainable creative ecosystems that uplift communities.

Our Mission

To empower youth through skill development, creative expression, mentorship, and access to corporate and global opportunities.

Our Core Values

  • Creativity: Original ideas and expression
  • Integrity: Honesty and transparency
  • Empowerment: Uplifting the next generation
  • Collaboration: Growing through partnerships
  • Excellence: Quality in everything we do
